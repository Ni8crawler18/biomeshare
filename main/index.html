    What is Biomeshare ?

    Biomeshare is a decentralized platform leveraging Ethereum
    blockchain for secure transactions, Chainlink for real-time data, GIS,
    and satellite imagery for project validation. Users can tokenize assets,
    create projects, and make micro-investments, fostering transparency
    and scalability in sustainable investing, addressing the challenge of limited
    investments in biodiversity and nature projects.
